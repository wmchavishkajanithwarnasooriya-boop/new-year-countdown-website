2025 Summary

A look back at the defining moments that shaped Sri Lanka this year.

January
01

Duruthu Perahera

The sacred Duruthu Perahera illuminated Kelaniya, marking a spiritual start to the year with grand processions.
February
02

National Day & Literature

Celebrated Independence Day with pride, while the Galle Literary Festival brought global intellects to the southern coast.
March
03

Science Summit

Colombo hosted a major International Science Summit, positioning Sri Lanka as a hub for regional innovation.
April
04

Avurudu Festivities

The island united for Sinhala & Tamil New Year, celebrating harvest and harmony with traditional games and feasts.
May
05

Vesak Illuminations

Cities glowed with lanterns and pandols during Vesak, attracting record numbers of tourists to witness the spectacle.
June
06

Poson Pilgrimage

Devotees flocked to Mihintale for Poson, reaffirming the island's deep-rooted Buddhist heritage.
July
07

Kataragama Esala

The multi-religious Kataragama Esala Festival showcased a vibrant blend of devotion, dance, and fire-walking traditions.
August
08

Kandy Esala Perahera

The majestic Kandy Perahera paraded the Sacred Tooth Relic, a dazzling display of culture viewed by millions globally.
September
09

Sports Triumphs

Sri Lankan athletes shone on the global stage at the Asian Youth Games, bringing home gold and glory.
October
10

Tech & Innovation

The National IT Conference in Colombo set new benchmarks for the country's digital future and tech exports.
November
11

Deepavali Lights

The Festival of Lights was celebrated with grandeur, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness across the island.
December
12

Year End Reflections
